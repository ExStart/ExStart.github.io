We're ExStart

ExStart is an effort to make contributing on Elixir projects easy and manageable for all. We do this by making well documented repositories with easy, clear, and well defined issues. DocTests help too.

What we've done

To date Elixir has created crutches. Crutches is a project to port over ActiveSupport from Ruby. In an effort to do so, we've found that following a simple recipe makes contributions to Elixir open-source repositories easy and fun to do. By outlining tasks through DocTests and making issues explicit, reasonable in scope, and clear, crutches was accessible to contributors with little experience.
